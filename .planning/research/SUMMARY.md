## Domain Ecosystem Summary
Flashcard apps prioritize speed of recall and distraction-free environments. The technical challenge lies in balancing a "premium" feel (smooth 3D animations) with low-latency data persistence. Using the MVVM + GetX pattern ensures that the animation logic stays separated from data management, preventing "spaghetti code" in large controllers.
